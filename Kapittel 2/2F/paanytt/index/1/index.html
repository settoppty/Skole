Overview

LittleBigPlanet is a 2008 platform video game developed by Media Molecule and published by Sony Computer Entertainment for the PlayStation 3. It is the first installment in the LittleBigPlanet series. In LittleBigPlanet, the player controls Sackboy, a customizable ragged doll. The game is primarily centered around content creation, with examples including a level editor and the Popit, a menu used for accessing creation tools.
